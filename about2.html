My Story

My Story

Hi there! I'm Terry Jeffery, the creator and maker behind ReVIVE9. What began as a creative outlet in my living room has grown into a handcrafted jewelry line built on creativity, intention, and everyday style.

Every piece in ReVIVE9 is designed and handmade by me—no mass production, no shortcuts, no outsourcing. Just thoughtful, unique jewelry crafted with care and a genuine love for the details.

My goal is simple: to elevate your everyday wear with pieces that feel special, effortless, and uniquely you. Whether you're dressing up or keeping it casual, I hope ReVIVE9 brings that perfect finishing touch to your look.

Thank you so much for supporting handmade and for being part of the ReVIVE9 journey

— Terry Jeffery
